What are MOOCs and edX?

MOOCs are Massive Open Online Courses. These courses can be taken wherever and whenever a learner wants.
A MOOC is the ideal way to teach a large global audience of learners.

The Open edX platform is the leading open-source MOOC platform, created by edX and its founding partners, MIT and Harvard, with contributions from countless partner universities.

OpenCraft’s Expertise

OpenCraft is one of the Open edX platform's contributors since the very first day the project was released as open source. OpenCraft has made hundreds of contributions to the Open edX platform and its plugin ecosystem (“XBlocks”). The OpenCraft and edX teams know each other and work well together, edX being both a client and a partner for OpenCraft.
